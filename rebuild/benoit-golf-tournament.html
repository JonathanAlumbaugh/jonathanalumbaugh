recently Jonathan overhauled Print collateral for the Matt T. Benoit Memorial Scholarship Fund's 11th annual golf classic. Given free reign to make nearly any change, a clean system that worked across all of the formats required for the tournament was quickly developed. Before and after the lockup was cleaned up. A clean cover was developed for the program (a simple 8.5x11 booklet) that incorporated subtle color. The system flowed from the design of the program to the tee signs, sponsor and corporate sponsor signs, and thank you note to the volunteers. A 24x36 inch corporate sponsor sign that was printed on coroplast. A list of sponsors was kept in an Excel doc, making it easy to set up an InDesign file for a datamerge, which resulted in about 40 different sponsor signs in a matter of seconds with minimal editing afterwards.

The client required the printed files in time for the event and factoring in print time, the amount of time to design came out to a day. All materials were designed, proofed, and sent to print on time.
